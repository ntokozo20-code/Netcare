Departments

At Netcare, our departments work together to deliver world-class healthcare with compassion and innovation. Each unit is dedicated to providing specialised medical services, ensuring that every patient receives the highest standard of care, safety, and support throughout their health journey.

  1. Home
  2. Departments

Cardiology

Heart & Vascular

Our Cardiology Department provides advanced diagnostic and treatment services for heart and vascular conditions. With cutting-edge technology and a team of leading specialists, we deliver personalised care to help our patients achieve optimal heart health and overall well-being.

24/7 Emergency Advanced Diagnostics

Neurology

Brain & Nervous System

Our Neurology Department offers expert care for conditions affecting the brain, spine, and nervous system. Using advanced diagnostic tools and innovative treatments, our specialists are committed to improving neurological health, restoring function, and enhancing quality of life for every patient.

MRI Available Specialist Team

Orthopedics

Bones & Joints

Our Orthopaedics Department specialises in the diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation of bone, joint, and muscle conditions. From sports injuries to complex surgeries, our team combines medical expertise with advanced technology to help patients regain mobility and live pain-free, active lives.

Sports Medicine Joint Replacement

Pediatrics

Children's Health

Our Paediatrics Department is dedicated to providing compassionate, specialised care for infants, children, and adolescents. With a focus on comfort, safety, and family support, our team ensures that every child receives the best possible medical attention in a warm and nurturing environment.

Child-Friendly Vaccination Center

Laboratory

Diagnostic Services

Our Laboratory Department provides accurate and timely diagnostic services to support effective patient care. Equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by experienced professionals, we deliver reliable results that aid in diagnosis, treatment planning, and monitoring of patient health.

Quick Results Digital Reports

Emergency

24/7 Critical Care

Our Emergency Department is available around the clock to provide immediate, life-saving care for critical and urgent medical situations. Staffed by highly trained specialists and supported by advanced equipment, we ensure rapid response, accurate treatment, and compassionate care when every second counts.

Round-the-Clock Trauma Center
